\# Key Insights from AI Job Market Analysis



\## Experience Level Demand

Mid-level (MI) roles appear most frequently, indicating strong hiring demand for professionals with some industry experience.



\## Salary Trends by Role

Machine Learning Engineers, AI Engineers, and ML Researchers show the highest average salary levels.



\## Remote Work Distribution

Fully remote (100%) opportunities form a significant portion of AI job postings, showing flexibility in modern AI roles.



\## Company Hiring Patterns

Large-sized companies contribute the majority of hiring volume in AI-related positions.



\## Salary Growth Over Time

Average salaries show an upward trend across recent years, reflecting increasing demand for AI professionals globally.



\## Entry-Level Opportunities

Entry-level (EN) roles exist but are fewer compared to mid-level roles, highlighting the importance of practical project experience.
